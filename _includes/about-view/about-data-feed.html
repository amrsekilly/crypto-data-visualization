About cryptocurrency data feed

The Cryptocurrency Data Feed, a partnership between Blockstream and Intercontinental Exchange (ICE), offers traders best in class real-time and historical cryptocurrency data from a strong and growing list of exchange partners worldwide.

  • X crypto and fiat currency pairs in the feed represent over Y trade and quote updates every day.
  • Crosses are captured and normalized which creates a unique sequence number, details on where the trade took place, and other relevant order book data such as quantity, price, currency and timestamp.
  • Level 2 order book data gives traders full-depth market-by-price by venue insight.
  • Value added calculated fields include Accumulated Volume, VWAP, Turnover Value, and Open/High/Low/Last at standard time 16:55 UTC.
  • Venue status messages indicate whether a venue is healthy or currently experiencing a technical interruption.

We believe that data transparency will attract more investors, and increase liquidity, price stability, and overall confidence in this new asset class.
