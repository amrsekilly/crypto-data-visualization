About cryptocurrency data feed

Blockstream's Cryptocurrency Data Feed (Crypto Feed) offers traders high quality, real-time cryptocurrency data from a strong and growing list of exchange partners globally, with prices and order book data for bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies. The multi-asset and multi-exchange data feed covers the majority of cryptocurrency exchange trading volume for a broad range of cryptocurrencies against the U.S. Dollar and other major currency pairs.
